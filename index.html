About Me

Hi. I am Vika Cherkeziya.
I was born in Russia, in 2000 on May 12th.
Before school, I also grew up in Russia, then moved to Georgia, Tbilisi, and since then I have been living and studying here.
After school, when it was time to go to university and choose my future profession, I was not sure what would bring me not only money, but most importantly, joy and a sense of freedom, work that I will do every day even without any income. I decided that until I find my inspiration, I will engage in self-development and give myself as much time as necessary.
Somehow I accidentally saw a video on the Internet where one developer explained how interesting and in demand the field of programming is in the modern world. After that, I started watching tutorials, reading books and absorbing information every day like a sponge, I was more and more interested.
After almost 2 months of self-study, I can say that the only thing I regret is that I did not know about this profession before, when I was supposed to enter the university, because I am sure that I want to do this, with pleasure, all my life.
